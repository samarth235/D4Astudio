Urban Edge

D4A Studio's proposal envisions an exclusive uber-luxury residential community in Sigehalli, crafted to offer calm,privacy, and sophistication. The architecture features contemporary tower forms, expansive decks, and a refined material palette that elevates modern living.Spacious 3,3.5 & 4,4.5 BHK residences, duplex sky villas, and penthouses are planned with generous ceiling heights and premium finishes. A resort-style clubhouse, wellness spa, temperature-controlled pool, and sky terraces create a lifestyle of comfort and indulgence.

Amarta

The Amarta Marketing Office by D4A STUDIO is not just a workspace; it's a strategically designed client experience. The project redefines the traditional corporate environment by integrating hospitality and engagement into its core. The design guides visitors through a seamless and engaging journey. The reception area sets a welcoming tone, leading into focused discussion rooms for detailed conversations. The central feature of the project is a captivating central courtyard. A tranquil water body surrounds a central tree, creating a peaceful oasis accessible to clients. This courtyard serves as a symbolic and functional focal point, reflecting the peaceful and community-oriented nature of the properties being marketed.

RRN LUX

D4A Studio presents an uber-luxury residential address in RR Nagar that blends timeless architecture with contemporary living. The design choreographs light, privacy, and landscape to create a calm, resort-grade,experience minutes from the city, yet worlds away from its pace.Set within a serene residential precinct of RR Nagar, the master plan orients towers to capture prevailing breezes and long views while minimizing heat gain and overlooking.Vehicular movement is pushed to the periphery, preserving a pedestrian-first central quiet interiors.

Suresh Residence

This is a design for a four-story, East-facing residential building on a 40' x 60' site in Sarjapur, created for the Suresh Residence. The architecture, by D4A STUDIO, combines modern aesthetics with functional spaces, focusing on natural light, open-plan living, and unique features like a double-height dining area and an integrated waterfall. The East-facing orientation is utilized to maximize morning sunlight ,creating bright and inviting living spaces. The design incorporates a setback for a small garden and sit-out area, positioned to catch the morning sun. The site dimensions are 40 feet in width and 60 feet in depth.

Sumukha Residence

This project, "Sumukha Residence," is a modern renovation of a residential building on a 30' x 50' East-facing site The design focuses on updating the existing structure to meet contemporary living standards,maximizing space, improving natural light and ventilation, and introducing a sleek, modern aesthetic. The renovation aims to create a more functional and elegant home without expanding the original footprint.
